How To Play

For mobile, tap the Smiley Face.
For PC, press Spacebar or click Mouse Button.
Choose one of three songs: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
Start tapping after you see the 3, 2, 1, GO prompt!
Watch the beat visual to see when to tap the beat.
Try and match the beats. The closer your match, the higher your score!
