How To Play

For mobile, tap the Smiley Face.
For PC, press Spacebar or click Mouse Button.

You will first hear a standard beat. Then, you need to tap/click for 16 beats.
Then you will hear a slow beat. Tap/click again for 16 beats.
Lastly, you will hear a fast beat. Match these beats the best you can.
Try and match the beats. The closer your match, the higher your score!
